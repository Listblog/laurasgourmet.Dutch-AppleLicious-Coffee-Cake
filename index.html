Dutch AppleLicious Coffee Cake Recipe
By Chef Laura Briscoe
Prep time: 15 minutes Cook time: 45 minutes Servings: 1 piece, serves 12

Ingredients

- 4 ounces butter, unsalted, plant based or dairy, room temperature

- 1 cup brown sugar, packed

- 1 large egg

- 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract

- 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour. 1:1 substitution using gluten free flour

- 1 T baking powder

- 1/2 teaspoon salt

- 6 ounces almond milk, unsweetened vanilla. Can substitute dairy milk

- 1/2 cup Laura's Gourmet Granola, AppleLicious Crunch

- 2 each Honeycrisp apples, peeled, cored, in 1/4 inch thick slices

Instructions

  1. Combine flour, baking powder and salt in a bowl and set aside.
  2. Cream butter with brown sugar until fluffy. Add egg and vanilla and beat well.
  3. Beginning with the flour mixture, add to the creamed ingredients, alternating with the milk and ending with the flour mixture.
  4. Prepare an oblong baking dish, 7 x 11 5 x 12 or other.
  5. Spread the batter into the pan.
  6. Sprinkle two thirds of the Â½ cup of granola over the batter.
  7. Layer the apples over the granola decoratively.
  8. Top the apples with the remaining granola.
  9. Place into a preheated 325 convection oven and bake for 45 minutes. Cool slightly and serve.

Notes
Note: AppleLicious Cake may be kept at room temperature, tightly wrapped, for up to three days.
